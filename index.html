Tech

A big part of NUCATS is educating people on the latest technologies and how to get the most out of them. We give sessions on getting started with various technologies including Android, Google Home and Arduino programming.

Discover more about tech with NUCATS

Gaming

As well as tech and social events, we also run frequent gaming evenings, where you can try out the latest consoles, race in VR and put your skills to the test in various tournaments.

Discover more about gaming with NUCATS

Social

University life can be tough and that’s why it’s important you have the opportunity to blow off steam. From regular bar crawls and pub quizzes to bowling and just meeting up for pizza, there will be plenty of opportunities to relax and let your hair down.

See future and past NUCATS events

Outreach

NUCATS run an Outreach teaching programme, endorsed by the School of Computing Science at Newcastle University. It gives young people the opportunity to get expert tuition on coding and basic computer science knowledge to help give them that extra step up into a rapidly advancing field.

Learn more about Outreach
